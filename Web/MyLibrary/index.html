Book Notes

▶Context

Characters

  • Dario Brando: Father of Dio, a rogue man who stole George Joestar in a carriage accident.
  • George Joestar: Father of Jonathan, Dio's adoptive father.
  • Mary Joestar: Mother of Jonathan, a woman who died in the carriage accident.
  • Jonathan Joestar (JoJo): a man who wants to be a squire and save people.
  • Erina Pendleton: a woman who is bullied by brats.
  • Dio Brando: a man who wants every piece of wealth and power in the world.

The Stone Mask Prologue

  • A man made a stone mask come alive by covering the mask with blood.
  • The mask pierced the brain of the wearer of the mask and gave him power and eternal life.
  • The Aztecs of the 12th and 16th century B.C. vanished from the center of Mexico.

Chapter 1: Dio the Invader

  • George thought Dario was saving him from a carriage accident, so he owes him.
  • Dario gives Dio a letter for George and Dio starts being cared of by the Joestar family.
  • Jonathan gets beaten trying to save Erina and Dio kicks Jonathan's dog.

Chapter 2: A New Friend!

  • Dio strives to be better than Jonathan and make him look bad in everything.
  • Dio bets his monthly allowance to beat Jonathan in boxing and beats him up.
  • Dio tells to everyone that he will teach them boxing moves and makes them hate JoJo.

Chapter 3: Beloved Erina

  • Jonathan feels friendless and Erina returns Jonathan his handkerchief in a basket with fruits.
  • Jonathan and Erina have fun until they fall in love, and JoJo carves their names into a tree.
  • Dio sees the heart carving of JoJo and Erina, so he kisses Erina in front of JoJo's old friends.

▶Context

Chapter 1: Moving

  • Yotsuba and Yousuke move in and Yotsuba gets lost when Yousuke and Jambo put the boxes.
  • Fuuka looks for Yotsuba after meeting Yousuke, and Yotsuba runs away from her.
  • Asagi protects Yotsuba from Fuuka; Asagi, Fuuka and Ena welcome Yotsuba and Yousuke.

▶Context

Introduction

  • Nick graduates in New Haven in 1915 and goes to fight in the Great War.
  • Nick goes to New York in 1922 during a summer to learn the bond business.
  • Nick starts living in West Egg (Long Island) next to a rich man's house, the house of Gatsby.

Chapter 1: Tom and Daisy

  • Nick goes to East Egg and has dinner with Daisy, Tom and Jordan.
  • Jordan tells Nick that Tom is having an affair with a woman in New York.
  • Nick returns to West Egg and sees Gatsby stretching his arms to a green light in the sea.
