Book Notes

▶Context

Characters

  • Dario Brando: Father of Dio, a rogue man who stole George Joestar in a carriage accident.
  • George Joestar: Father of Jonathan, Dio's adoptive father.
  • Mary Joestar: Mother of Jonathan, a woman who died in the carriage accident.
  • Jonathan Joestar (JoJo): a man who wants to be a squire and save people.
  • Erina Pendleton: a woman who is bullied by brats.
  • Dio Brando: a man who wants every piece of wealth and power in the world.

The Stone Mask Prologue

  • A man made a stone mask come alive by covering the mask with blood.
  • The mask pierced the brain of the wearer of the mask and gave him power and eternal life.
  • The Aztecs of the 12th and 16th century B.C. vanished from the center of Mexico.

Chapter 1: Dio the Invader

  • George thought Dario was saving him from a carriage accident, so he owes him.
  • Dario gives Dio a letter for George and Dio starts being cared of by the Joestar family.
  • Jonathan gets beaten trying to save Erina and Dio kicks Jonathan's dog.

Chapter 2: A New Friend!

  • Dio strives to be better than Jonathan and make him look bad in everything.
  • Dio bets his monthly allowance to beat Jonathan in boxing and beats him up.
  • Dio tells to everyone that he will teach them boxing moves and makes them hate JoJo.

Chapter 3: Beloved Erina

  • Jonathan feels friendless and Erina returns Jonathan his handkerchief in a basket with fruits.
  • Jonathan and Erina have fun until they fall in love, and JoJo carves their names into a tree.
  • Dio sees the heart carving of JoJo and Erina, so he kisses Erina in front of JoJo's old friends.

Chapter 4: A Fight I Munstn't Lose

  • Erina washes her face in the gutter and Dio slaps her face.
  • George tells Dio how Danny saved JoJo from drowning and Erina runs out when she sees JoJo.
  • Jonathan finds out what Dio has done and beats him until he cries as revenge.

Chapter 5: Danny in Flames

  • The stone mask awakens to Dio's blood, as he cries out to JoJo with a hidden knife.
  • George stops the fight by sending the two to their room, and later Dio incinerates Danny.
  • After Danny's death, Jonathan grieves while unable to speak to Erina, and seven years pass.

Chapter 6: A Letter from the Past

  • Jonathan and Dio win a rugby match and rush home to tell their sick dad.
  • Jonathan investigates the stone mask with his archeological notes and discovers Dario's letter.
  • Dio poisons George's medicine, and JoJo asks him about George's symptoms resembling Dario's.

Chapter 7: A Vow to Father

  • Dio bought medicine for Dario, but he told him to buy booze with mother's dress instead.
  • Dio bought poison for George from the same oriental man who sold him poison for Dario.
  • Jonathan takes the poison from Dio and throws him off the top railing to the ground.

Chapter 8: Battle on Ogre Street

  • Jonathan tells George not to take any medicine from anyone, as he is going away for three days.
  • Dio breaks into JoJo's drawer and obtains the stone mask, intending to use it to kill JoJo.
  • Jonathan fights off two of three thugs on Ogre Street and asks where they sell eastern poison.

▶Context

Characters

  • Speedwagon: a thug from Ogre Street with a flying saw hat, he fights Jonathan.

Chapter 9: The Live Subject Test of the Mask

  • Speedwagon, the third thug, strikes JoJo's arm with his flying saw hat, but JoJo kicks him.
  • Speedwagon recognizes JoJo as a gentleman and offers to help him find the poison dealer.
  • Dio gets in a fight with some hooligans on Ogre Street and tests the stone mask on one of them.

▶Context

Characters

  • Yotsuba Koiwai: Little girl.
  • Yousuke Koiwai: Father of Yotsuba.
  • Takashi Takeda (Jambo): Friend of Yousuke.
  • Asagi Ayase: Oldest sister.
  • Fuuka Ayase: Middle sister.
  • Ena Ayase: Youngest sister.

Chapter 1: Moving

  • Yotsuba and Yousuke move in and Yotsuba gets lost when Yousuke and Jambo put the boxes.
  • Fuuka looks for Yotsuba after meeting Yousuke, and Yotsuba runs away from her.
  • Asagi protects Yotsuba from Fuuka; Asagi, Fuuka and Ena welcome Yotsuba and Yousuke.

▶Context

Characters

  • Nick Carraway: protagonist.
  • Jay Gatsby: Nick's right hand neighbor.
  • Daisy: Nick's cousin
  • Tom Buchanan: Daisy's husband, a rich man and a famous football player.
  • Jordan Baker: a woman golfer that appears on the newspapers.

Introduction

  • Nick graduates in New Haven in 1915 and goes to fight in the Great War.
  • Nick goes to New York in 1922 during a summer to learn the bond business.
  • Nick starts living in West Egg (Long Island) next to a rich man's house, the house of Gatsby.

Chapter 1: Tom and Daisy

  • Nick goes to East Egg and has dinner with Daisy, Tom and Jordan.
  • Jordan tells Nick that Tom is having an affair with a woman in New York.
  • Nick returns to West Egg and sees Gatsby stretching his arms to a green light in the sea.
